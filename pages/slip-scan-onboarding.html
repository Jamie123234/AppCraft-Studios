Interactive Onboarding

Follow these steps to set up your account, create or join an organization, and start scanning.

1

Step 1: Installation & Registration

Download Slip-Scan on iOS or Android.

  • Open the app and Register (or Log In).
  • Once logged in, select the Business tab (do not stay on Personal).
Currency: Default is USD. Go to the Account Page now and set your preferred currency.
2

Step 2: Choose Your Path

A. Create Organization

For Business Owners/Admins.

  • 1. Select "Create Organization".
  • 2. Enter Company Name.
  • 3. Configure Tax Settings (Dynamic AI, VAT, or GST).

B. Join Organization

For Managers & Employees.

  • 1. Select "Join Existing Organization".
  • 2. You will be prompted for an Invite Code.
  • 3. Wait for your invite email (see Step 3).
3

Step 3: Building the Team (Owner Only)

The Owner must initiate the invites from the Team Page.

  1. Navigate to the Team Page.
  2. Click the icon in the Top Right Corner to Invite New Member.
  3. Select Role: Manager first, then Employees.
  4. Enter Email: CRITICAL You must enter the exact email the user will register with. If the emails do not match, they cannot join.
  5. The system sends an email with a unique Invite Code to the user.
4

Step 4: Entering the Code

Once the Manager or Employee receives the email:

  • Copy the Invite Code from the email.
  • Paste it into the "Join Organization" prompt in the app.
  • The app will load your specific Variant (Employee App or Manager App).
5

Step 5: Assignments & Workspaces

Before employees can scan effectively, the Owner must configure the structure.

Assign Managers

When creating Employees, the Owner assigns them to a specific Manager.

Assign Workspaces

On the Team Page, the Owner clicks on a Manager to assign Clients, Projects, Departments, and Tags. These filter down to the employees managed by that person.

Team Roles & Hierarchy

Slip-Scan Business uses a 3-tier hierarchy to ensure security and efficient delegation.

The Owner

The creator of the organization. Has full administrative control over all business operations.

Key Capabilities:

  • Business Dashboard: Complete collective business analytics.
  • Advanced Reports: Full financial oversight.
  • Management: Manage Teams, Roles, Subscriptions & Workspaces.
  • Tax Setup: Sets Dynamic AI, VAT, or GST.
  • Approve All: Can toggle "Approve All" to override managers.

Manager

Assigned to oversee specific employees and act as approval gatekeepers.

Key Capabilities:

  • Personal Analytics: View stats for their own scans.
  • Approval Page: Approve or Reject team receipts.
  • Workspaces: Use workspaces assigned by Owner.
  • No access to collective Business Dashboard.

Employee

The primary users capturing data. They must be assigned to a manager.

Key Capabilities:

  • Scan & AI Extraction.
  • Inbox Swipe: Swipe to submit for approval.
  • View personal analytics.
  • Rejection: Must redo scan from scratch if rejected.

Approval Workflow

The lifecycle of an expense from camera click to accounting ledger.

1. Capture & Pre-Population

Employee takes a photo. The app uses AI to extract Vendor, Date, Total, and Tax.

Pre-Population Summary

Immediately during the scanning process (before submission), the user can select the correct Department, Project, Client, and Tags.

Batch Scan: Scan multiple receipts in one session.
PDF Support: Upload multi-page PDFs for long invoices.

2. Submission

The receipt is saved as a Draft. In the Inbox, the user simply swipes the card to Submit. Since all tags were added during capture, no further editing is required.

3. Manager Review

The assigned manager sees the scan in their Approval Page.

Approve

Moves the receipt to the final ledger.

Reject

Strict Policy: If rejected, the employee must redo the entire scan from scratch.

4. Finalization

Approved receipts are locked. They appear in the Business Analytics dashboard (Owner only).

Workspace Configuration

Customize Slip-Scan to match your company's internal structure.

Owner Control: Only Owners can create these categories. They must then be assigned to Managers via the Team Page for them to be active.

Projects

Create project codes (e.g., "Project Alpha"). Managers receive these assignments and employees select them when scanning.

Clients

Track billable expenses. Tag receipts to specific clients for reinvoicing reports.

Departments

Structure spending by internal function (Marketing, IT, Sales). Essential for budgeting.

Custom Tags

Ad-hoc labeling. Example: #HolidayParty2025.

Analytics & Reports

For Owners: Business Analytics

The only role with complete collective business oversight. View total Income, Expenses, charts, and metrics across all Workspaces.

For Managers

Access to Personal Analytics only. Track your own submissions and reimbursement status.
