Privacy Policy for Slip-Scan

Last Updated: October 15, 2025

AppCraft Studios (Pty) Ltd ("us", "we", or "our") operates the Slip-Scan mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

Information Collection and Use

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

Permissions and Data Usage

To provide its core functionality, Slip-Scan requests access to the following permissions:

Camera Access

Purpose: We request access to your device's camera to allow you to capture images of your receipts, invoices, and other financial documents. This is a primary feature of the app, enabling you to quickly scan and digitize your expenses without manual entry.
Example: A user opens the "Capture Invoice" feature in the app, which activates the camera so they can take a picture of a restaurant bill to log it as an expense.

Photo Library Access

Purpose: We request access to your photo library to allow you to upload existing images of receipts and invoices. This is for your convenience if you have already taken a picture of a receipt and want to upload it to Slip-Scan for processing.
Example: A user selects "Select From Gallery" to choose a photo of a receipt they received via email and saved to their device's photo library.

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data

While using our Service, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information ("Personal Data"). This may include, but is not limited to: Email address, First name and last name, and any data extracted from the documents you scan (such as vendor names, dates, and amounts).

Usage Data

We may also collect information on how the Service is accessed and used ("Usage Data"). This may include your device's IP address, device type, operating system version, and other diagnostic data to help us improve the app.

Use of Data

AppCraft Studios (Pty) Ltd uses the collected data to provide and maintain our Service, to process your scanned documents, to notify you about changes, to provide customer support, and to monitor the usage of our Service.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
