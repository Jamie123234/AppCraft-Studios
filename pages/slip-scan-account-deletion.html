Delete Your Slip-Scan Account

We're sorry to see you go. You can delete your account and all associated data using one of the methods below.

Option 1: Delete From Within the App (Recommended)

The fastest and most secure way to delete your account is directly within the Slip-Scan app.

  1. Open the Slip-Scan app on your mobile device.
  2. Navigate to your Profile or Account Settings page.
  3. Find and tap the "Delete Account" option.
  4. Follow the on-screen prompts to confirm the deletion.

Please note: This action is irreversible and will permanently delete all your scanned receipts, reports, and account data.

Option 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you are unable to access the app, you can submit a deletion request via email. For security, please send this request from the email address associated with your Slip-Scan account.

Clicking this button will open your default email client with a pre-filled message. Our support team will process your request within 48 hours.
