1. Server Infrastructure & Backup Protocol

We utilize a managed Platform-as-a-Service (PaaS) infrastructure powered by Supabase, which is hosted on Amazon Web Services (AWS) enterprise-grade data centers.

Infrastructure: Our database and backend services are hosted in AWS regions compliant with SOC2, HIPAA, and ISO27001 standards.

Backup Frequency: We maintain automated daily full backups of all production databases.

Storage: Backups are encrypted at rest and stored in geographically redundant AWS Availability Zones to ensure data survival in the event of a physical data center failure.

2. Disaster Recovery & Business Continuity

Server-Side Recovery (RTO): In the event of a server-side disruption (e.g., database connectivity or API failure) within our control, we aim to restore backend service functionality within 4 to 24 hours.

Client-Side Updates (Platform Dependency): For critical issues requiring an update to the mobile application binary (APK/IPA), the timeline for resolution is subject to the review and approval processes of third-party platforms (Google Play Store and Apple App Store).

Disclaimer: AppCraft Studios cannot guarantee specific turnaround times for updates that require App Store approval. We commit to submitting the critical patch to the relevant Store within 24 hours of resolution, but the "Go Live" time is strictly dependent on the Platform's review queue.

Third-Party Dependency (Force Majeure): The Service relies on the Supabase platform (hosted on AWS). In the event of a catastrophic failure, discontinuation, or extended outage of the upstream provider (Supabase/AWS), recovery timelines will be subject to the provider's restoration schedule. AppCraft Studios is not liable for downtime caused strictly by third-party infrastructure outages.

Recovery Point Objective (RPO): Our backup frequency ensures that in a standard recovery scenario (restoring from the previous night's backup), data loss will not exceed 24 hours.

3. Data Privacy & Third-Party Disclosure

Sub-Processors: Our core infrastructure provider is Supabase (running on AWS). We do not grant these providers access to view raw data; they act solely as infrastructure hosts.

No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, trade, or transfer Client Data to external parties for marketing, advertising, or data mining purposes. Data is used solely for the provision of the agreed-upon service.

4. Data Retention Policy

Active Term: Data is retained indefinitely while the client holds an active account/contract to facilitate service usage.

Termination: Upon contract termination, data is retained in a dormant state for 30 days to facilitate potential reactivation or data export requests.

Purge: After the 30-day retention period, all Client Data is permanently and irretrievably deleted from our live production databases.
